Our Story

Welcome to Cara Mia, where every dish tells a story of passion, tradition, and family.

Cara Mia was born to bring the authentic flavors of Italy to Rancho Cucamonga. Our founder, poured his heart and soul into creating a place where food isn't just sustenance, but an experience that brings people together.

Every recipe, every ingredient, every moment spent in our kitchen carries his spirit and dedication. His family continue his legacy by serving dishes made with the same love and attention to detail that he brought to every plate.

"In loving memory of our founder, whose passion for Italian cuisine and love for everyone continues to inspire us every day."
